How it all started.

Coming from a business-oriented family, I always knew that a conventional 9-to-6 job wasn’t for me. With a deep passion for visual storytelling, I moved to Mumbai to pursue cinematography. The city of dreams not only honed my craft but also taught me independence, resilience, and the courage to follow what I love.

My journey began in 2013 as a freelance cinematographer, capturing moments through photo walks and portrait sessions. It was during this time that I received my first call for a wedding project. That experience sparked a realization—I could turn my passion into a profession.

In 2015, with the drive to grow and refine my skills, I officially stepped into the world of wedding cinematography. Starting with my first wedding in my hometown, the journey since then has been nothing short of extraordinary.

With over 150 weddings captured across the country, I’ve been fortunate to travel to incredible destinations, preserving emotions and timeless moments. My love for storytelling through films and cinematography continues to inspire me, making every frame a piece of art and every wedding a new story to tell.
